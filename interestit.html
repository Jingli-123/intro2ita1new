Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

When I decided to study in Bachelor of Information Technology, I considered few things to choose the university I like. Firstly, it must in a top of university group in Melbourne and it close my home. Secondly, it has online study course as I am a single mother, I must work full time to keep my family running normally in the first two years. Lastly, it has advantage in Computer Science or Information Technology course. Based on those, I chose RMIT. Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology is one of the top public research universities in Melbourne. RMIT is ranked 190th in Quacquarelli Symonds 2023 and 244th globally in Best Global Universities 2022. The main campus of RMIT is situated on the northern edge of the historic Hoddle Grid in the city center of Melbourne. The buildings of RMIT are very famous in Melbourne city, and they all look like a beautiful artwork.
